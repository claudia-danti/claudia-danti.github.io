About me

I am currently a PhD student at the Center for Star and Planet Formation at the
University of Copenhagen, under the supervision of Dr. Michiel Lambrecht.s
Before moving to Copenhagen I completed my master project at MPIA Heidelberg,
working with Prof. Bertram Bitsch. My research currently focuses on investigating how the formation of gas giants
in the outer disc may affect (or even hinder) the formation of Earth-like planets.
This will hopefully help us better understand and constrain the mechanism of planet formation
and possibly explain what is the main cause of the differences between
our Solar System and the exoplanetary systems that we observe.

Research and publications

My main research interest lies in the topic of planet formation. I approach it through numerical
simulations that I subsequently compare to observations.
